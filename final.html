Welcome to my CSC106 Final Project Page!

This is my final project for my CSC106 class at URI. I worked with a partner to create an educational drawing app.
Through this app, the user will be able to learn how to draw all the letters of the alphabet by clicking on the desired letter,
choosing a color, then clicking and dragging with the mouse to draw. We hope this app will make learning the alphabet a bit more exciting and colorful!
